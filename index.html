OTP Date

Buyer pays the option fee — 1% of the purchase price — to book the unit. This grants the buyer the right, but not the obligation, to buy.

OTP Exercise Date

Buyer exercises the option by paying the remaining 4% (bringing the total deposit to 5%), converting the booking into a binding sale and purchase agreement. Standard: 2 weeks from OTP date.

Completion Date

Legal completion — the balance purchase price is paid and keys are handed over to the buyer. Standard: 10 weeks from the exercise date.

Extension Date

Optional. Allows the seller to remain in the property for an agreed period after completion., usually in exchange for compensation or lowering of the property price

Standard terms shown (2 weeks to exercise, 10 weeks to completion) are common market convention for Singapore resale property and are not fixed by law — actual periods are negotiated and stated in the Option to Purchase. Always verify against the signed OTP and your conveyancing lawyer's advice.
